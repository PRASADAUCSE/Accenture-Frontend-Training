ntroduction
 
Bubble Consult is a progressive business consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions and strategies to help businesses scale and thrive. As part of their growth strategy, they needed a modern, responsive
website that would enhance their online presence, engage their audience, and streamline client interactions.
 
Objectives
 
 Business Scaling: Develop a website that supports Bubble Consult’s goals for scaling their business by providing a strong digital platform to attract and retain clients.
 
 Responsive Design: Ensure the website performs seamlessly across all devices, including mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.
 
 Dynamic Testimonials Carousel: Implement a carousel feature in the website to show the client testimonials
 
 Contact Form Integration: Create an intuitive contact form to facilitate easy communication between potential clients and Bubble Consult.
 
User Story #1
 
Title: Scalable business website
 
Description: 
• As an owner,
• I want to have a website designed to support and facilitate business growth,
• So that the website can effectively offer enhanced features and help attract and convert more customers as the business expands.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
• The website should load quickly and efficiently, with optimized images, scripts, and other resources to ensure a smooth user experience
• The website should include effective lead generation features such as contact forms, social media links, and subscription forms
 
User Story #2
 
Title: Responsive website
 
Description: 
• As an owner,
• I want to have a website that provides a consistent and optimized user experience across all devices (mobile phones, tablets, and desktops),
• So that my customers can easily access and interact with the site regardless of the device they are using.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
• The website should automatically adjust its layout and content based on the screen size and orientation of the device.
• The design should be fluid and adaptable, ensuring that content is displayed properly on small screens (mobile phones), medium screens (tablets), and large screens (desktops).
 
User Story #3
 
Title: Carousel in website
 
Description: 
• As a product owner,
• I want to feature a carousel displaying customer testimonials on my website,
• So that I can effectively showcase positive feedback and build credibility with potential customers.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
• Users should be able to manually navigate through testimonials using next and previous buttons.
• Testimonials should also automatically rotate at an interval (e.g., every 5 seconds)
 
User Story #4
 
Title: Contact form for user queries
 
Description: 
• As a product owner,
• I want to provide a contact form on my website,
• So that users can easily submit their queries or messages directly through the website.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
 
• Implement form validation to ensure that all required fields are correctly filled out and that the email address is in the proper format
• After submission, display a confirmation message or thank-you note to the user indicating that their message has been received.
